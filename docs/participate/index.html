Participate in the W3C Web of Things

The W3C Web of Things (WoT) is defined and refined by experts worldwide. Get involved by joining the Working Group to drive standards, or the Community Groups to discuss with adopters and incubate ideas.

Working Group (WG)

The core of the standardization effort. Join the WG to develop normative specifications, create test suites, and coordinate all Task Forces.

Community Groups (CG)

Open forums designed to accelerate adoption, gather use cases, and facilitate focused discussions on specific domains or regions.
