Participate in the W3C Web of Things

The W3C Web of Things (WoT) is defined and refined by experts worldwide. Get involved by joining our Working Group to drive standards, or our Community Groups to discuss and share use cases.

Working Group (WG) & Standardization

The core of our standardizing effort. Join the WG to push use cases, develop normative specifications and test suites on the W3C Recommendation Track, and ensure royalty-free Web standards. All Task Forces and Interest Groups are coordinated here.

Community Groups (CG)

Open forums designed to accelerate adoption, gather use cases, and facilitate focused discussions on specific domains or regions.
