Participate in the W3C Web of Things

The W3C Web of Things (WoT) is defined, implemented, and refined by experts from all over the world. Get involved by joining our working group, participating in task forces, or attending our events and meetings.

Working Group (WG)

Develops normative specifications and test suites on the W3C Recommendation Track to ensure royalty-free Web standards

Interest Group (IG)

Explores new use cases, coordinates PlugFests to validate assumptions, and identifies mature work for the Working Group

Community Groups (CG)

Open forums designed to accelerate adoption, gather use cases, and facilitate focused discussions on specific domains or regions

Task Forces

Specialized sub-divisions focusing on discrete technical deliverables like Thing Descriptions or Security

Events

Face-to-face meetings, PlugFests, testfests, and ecosystem conferences happening across the globe

Meetings

Weekly online calls covering the active work items across timezones to ensure global participation
